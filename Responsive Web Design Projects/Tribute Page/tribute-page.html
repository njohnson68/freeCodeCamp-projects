Pelé

Hero, Icon, Legend

Here's a timeline of Pelé's life:

  • 1940 - Born in Três Corações, Minas Gerais, Brazil
  • 1956 - Began playing for Santos
  • 1957 - Began playing for Brazil national team
  • 1958 - Won first World Cup
  • 1962 - Won second World Cup
  • 1970 - Won third World Cup
  • 1975 - Began playing for New York Cosmos
  • 1995 - Became Brazil's inaugural Minister of Sports
  • 2022 - Died at the age of 82
